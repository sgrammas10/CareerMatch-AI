About CareerMatch AI

CareerMatch AI is a career development platform that helps job seekers find ideal roles and companies based on skills, values, and personality. By integrating resumes, performance reviews, and psychometric assessments, we build personalized profiles to connect talent with opportunity.

Our platform features a gamified swiping interface, enabling users to interact with job listings in a fun, intuitive way. Right swipes notify companies of interest, creating a dynamic, two-way discovery process.

Virtual or live intake sessions with a career coach or AI assistant help refine each profile, diving deeper into a user’s goals and experience to enhance job matching.

At its core, CareerMatch AI uses advanced machine learning to align candidates with jobs and company cultures where they can grow and thrive.
